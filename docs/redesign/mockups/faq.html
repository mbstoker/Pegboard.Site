Frequently asked questions

The things club organisers ask us most - getting started, what it costs, how the ratings work, and what happens to your data.

Getting started
Is there really a free trial?

Yes. The web app is free for at least 60 days, with no card details needed up front. You can run real club nights during the trial - it is the full product, not a cut-down demo. We will always tell you before the free period ends.

Do we need to install anything?

No. The web app runs in any modern browser - just sign in at play.epegboard.com and you are away. There is also a Windows desktop app for venues with unreliable internet, but most clubs never need it.

How long does it take to set up?

You can be running your first session in minutes. Sign in, add players as they arrive - regulars, drop-ins and last-minute changes - and let ePegboard build balanced games. There is nothing to configure before your first night.

What devices does it work on?

The session display is designed for a tablet or laptop at the desk - a screen everyone can gather around. Players can check their own profile, rating and match history on their phone. A larger screen for the courts is optional but works well.

Pricing & plans
What happens after the free trial?

You will have plenty of notice before the free period ends, and no charge happens automatically. Full pricing for the web app is on the Pricing page. [Placeholder - exact ongoing pricing to be confirmed.]

What is the difference between the web app and the desktop app?

The web app runs in any browser on any device, needs no install, and is where new features land first. The Windows desktop app runs fully offline - the right choice if your venue has unreliable internet - and costs £60 per year per club after a 60-day free trial.

Is the desktop app still supported?

Yes. Clubs that rely on it for offline venues can keep using it. New development focuses on the web app, so that is where the latest picking, competitions and analytics features appear first.

How it works
How does the rating system work?

Every player has a rating that reflects how they perform against expectation - not just who won. A narrow loss to a stronger pair can still move you up; a heavy loss to weaker players costs more. Ratings update after every game, and the same number quietly powers smart picking, handicap tournaments, partnership analytics and league selection.

Will it actually make our games fairer?

That is the whole point. ePegboard balances games on current form, tracks who has been sitting out, and keeps an eye on court-time fairness across the night - so stronger and newer players both get good games, and nobody is stuck waiting. It also shows exactly why each game was picked.

Can players see their own stats?

Yes. Each player has a profile - rating, win rate and recent match history - that they can check on their phone. Many clubs find the live ratings become a talking point in their own right.

Your club & your data
Can we get our data out?

Yes. Your club's data is yours. Reports - attendance, payments, members and more - can be exported to CSV whenever you need them, so the numbers are never locked in.
