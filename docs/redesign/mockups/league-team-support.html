Partnership analytics

Discover which partnerships actually work

Every pairing is measured against what their ratings predicted. See who consistently performs better together - and who only looks good on paper.

  • A "vs expected" figure for every pair
  • Over-performing or under-performing, at a glance
  • Confidence based on games played together

"Over-performing" means a pair regularly beats what their ratings expect. Those are the partnerships worth keeping together for league nights.

Player ratings

Ratings you can trust, from real results

Every game updates a player's rating based on how they performed against expectation - not just wins and losses. It is the foundation for fair pairings and balanced teams.

  • Stronger opponents are worth more
  • Narrow defeats hurt less than thrashings
  • Updates automatically after every game
